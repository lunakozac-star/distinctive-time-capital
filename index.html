Questions

Frequently asked questions

What types of watches qualify?

We focus on luxury watches with strong resale demand, especially Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Omega, Cartier, and other established brands.

Can I sell my watch outright?

Yes. You can request a direct cash offer and sell your watch if you are ready to move forward.

Can I buy my watch back later?

For qualifying watches, Distinctive Time Capital may offer a structured buyback option with clear written terms and an agreed time frame.

How fast can I get paid?

Same-day options may be available after review, authentication, agreement, and approval.

Do I need box and papers?

Box and papers can help valuation, but they are not always required. Each watch is reviewed individually.

Is this the same as a pawn shop?

No. Distinctive Time Capital is focused specifically on luxury timepieces and provides a private, watch-market-informed process.

Is my watch stored securely?

Watches accepted under a buyback arrangement are handled securely according to the agreed process and terms.

Are all watches approved?

No. Approval depends on brand, model, condition, documentation, market demand, and valuation.

Where do you operate?

We serve clients across Canada, including the Vancouver and Toronto areas. Private appointments are available on request.
